Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

CGI Wilkes Rebbe ("we," "us," or "our") operates the camp registration application at cgikingston.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you register for camp or create an account, we may collect:

  • Parent/guardian name and email address (via Google Sign-In or email registration)
  • Phone number and emergency contact information
  • Child's name, date of birth, gender, grade, and t-shirt size
  • Medical and allergy information relevant to your child's safety at camp
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe — we do not store credit card numbers)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process camp registrations and assign children to the appropriate division
  • Communicate with parents about camp schedules, updates, and emergencies
  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Ensure the health and safety of campers

Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account. We use this solely to authenticate your identity and create your account. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Camp staff who need it to ensure your child's safety and wellbeing
  • Stripe, our payment processor, to process transactions
  • Supabase, our database provider, which stores data securely

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access to personal information is limited to authorized camp administrators.

Data Retention

We retain registration data for the duration of the camp season and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

We collect children's information only as provided by their parent or guardian for the purpose of camp registration. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
